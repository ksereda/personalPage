ABOUT ME



Java Developer


My name is Kirill Sereda. I live in Minsk. I'm 28 years old. I was born April 29, 1989. I worked as a system administrator for 5 years and 2 months in a network of Victoria hotels in Minsk. It was a hotel Victoria 1, Victoria SPA, Victoria on Zamkova (located near Nemiga metro station) and Victoria Business Center. In my free time I like music and sport. I used to play guitar for 18 years. I started learning to play guitar when I was still in junior high school. I studied at the Belarusian State Communication Academy (formerly the Belarusian State College of Communications).

I studied at the Faculty of Telecommunications. I had a specialty is technical operation of telecommunications networks. I have a higher education. In the academy, I studied for 7 years - 4 years in the secondary specialty and 3 years at the highest. After college I enrolled in the 3 course of the institute. At my last job my duties included: service of the network between hotels, offices, uninterrupted work wi-fi for hotel guests, also I set up and administered servers (virtual and physical), PC users, network equipment (Cisco, Alcatel, HP), CCTV, interactive television and telephone communication, and much more.

Last year I took courses Java Developer on the Java Core and Java web in the Khasang company. I was trained in teamwork. We had a project based on the service delivery of goods and services "Delivery". It was a large project with the ability to authorize and access rights based on roles (customer, performer), with the possibility of placing an order and choosing among performers based on reviews and ratings, contacting the performer through an internal chat, paying for an order using bank cards, e-wallets, integration with Yandex cards and the ability to track order status (payment, loading, delivery , receipt of an order).

For today I'm doing an internship in a devincubator in Minsk. I'm participating in a team project to develop a web service for testing, written in the Java programming language. This is a full-featured Maven project. Stack of used technologies: Spring, Hibernate, MySQL, MVC, JDBC, REST and SOAP services, JSP, JavaScript, JUnit, Log4j, Tomcat, AJAX.
