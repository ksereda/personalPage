Delivery service



Click on the images to make them bigger



WEB-application of delivery service "Delivery":

- With authorization and access rights based on roles (customer, performer)
- Ability to place an order and choose among performers based on reviews and ratings
- Contacting the performer via the internal chat
- Payment by bank cards, electronic wallet
- Integration with Yandex maps and the ability to track the status of the order (payment, loading, delivery, receipt of the order).

This is a full-featured Maven project
Stack of used technologies: Spring, Hibernate, PostgreSQL, MVC, JDBC, JMS, REST and SOAP services, JSP, JavaScript, JUnit, Log4j, Tomcat, AJAX
